# Epic 1: Model Selection and Architecture

## Objective

This epic focuses on selecting suitable AI models and designing the foundational architecture of the EduGenie Learning Assistant.

## Selected AI Models

### Gemini 1.5 Pro

Gemini 1.5 Pro is selected for:

- Question Answering
- Summarization
- Quiz Generation
- Learning Recommendations

It provides strong reasoning, contextual understanding, structured response generation, and cloud-based AI inference.

### LaMini-Flan-T5-783M

LaMini-Flan-T5-783M is selected for:

- Concept Explanation

It is lightweight, CPU-compatible, and useful for generating simple beginner-friendly explanations.
